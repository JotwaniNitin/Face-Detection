About Us

What we do Our mission Our goal

Offer Reliable Attendance Platform

We offer a reliable attendance platfrom wherein with a click of button multiple students can mark themselves present without the hassle of a long tedious roll call.

Facial recognition is not only speedy but is absolutely foolproof. Moreover, it cuts down paper work and is another forward step in digitilisation

Exceptional Tech Interactivity

Our mission is to bring this new age technological feature to schools and colleges across the globe helping classrooms to get to learning in a flash.

Help Our Users

We want to help our users with a secure and foolproof platform for attendance. We provide interactive technologies for both students and teachers alike.
